Simple Vanilla Cake

Ingridients (for a medium cake)

  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (190g) all purpose flour
  • 1 and 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) milk

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 175 degrees celcius.Grease and flour a cake pan (round or square).
  2. Cream butter and Sugarin a bowl until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Add eggsone at a time, beating well. Then stir in vanilla.
  4. Mix dry ingredients(flour+baking powder) in another bowl.
  5. Combinedry mixture with the wet mixture, a little at a time.
  6. Stir in milk until the batter is smooth.
  7. Pour batterinto your prepared cake pan.
  8. Bakefor about 30-35 minutes,or until a tooth pick comes out clean.
  9. Cool the cake for 10 minutes before removing from pan.
  10. EnjoyYou can eat it plain or add frosting
